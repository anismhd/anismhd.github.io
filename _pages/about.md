## What I Do
My expertise lies in ensuring that high-stakes facilities, particularly Nuclear Power Plants, can withstand the most extreme environmental demands. I specialize in:

Probabilistic Seismic Hazard Analysis (PSHA): Developing frameworks to quantify ground motion risks using custom-built tools in MATLAB and Python.

Soil-Structure Interaction (SSI): Modeling how complex structures behave in tandem with the ground they sit on using specialized software like ACS SASSI.

Advanced Finite Element Analysis (FEA): Utilizing ABAQUS and ANSYS to solve non-linear structural challenges.

Regulatory Guidance: Translating complex engineering data into industry standards and safety guidelines.
